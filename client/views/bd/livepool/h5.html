The Global Traders League Rules
How to enter:
New Clients:
  • 1. Register and activate the League account.
  • 2. Virtual funds will then become available to the trader.
  • 3. Traders can monitor their positions on the league table 5 days after the competition’s launch.
Existing clients:
  • 1. All traders need to open a league account.
  • 2. Traders must open some trades on their league account
  • 3. Traders can monitor their positions on the league table 5 days after the competition’s launch.
The Global Traders League rules:
  • 1. The client must open a League account in order to participate.
  • 2. The client must open a position on the League account in order to be eligible.
  • 3. Participants can monitor their rankings in real-time, on a separate landing page.
  • 4. The top-ranked, most profitable trader based on the highest net worth will receive the winning prize.
  • 5. Two runners-up will receive runner-up prizes at TigerWit’s discretion.
Trading and Ranking Rules:
  • The system will start to record the results from 0:00 (GMT+4 )1st of October to 12:00PM (GMT+4) 31st of October.
  • All trades must be closed before the competition ends. The final profit result will not include any open positions.
