Earning commission

TigerWit Masters earn 20% of the value of successful trades made by followers. This amount is credited to the Master’s account automatically.

Paying commission

Followers pay 20% of the profit made by following a Master’s trade to the Master. Commission is deducted automatically

Unfollow

If you choose to unfollow a Master’s trade your position will remain open until it is manually closed or you choose ‘Unfollow and force-flat’. You will still be liable for 20% commission payable to the Master when the position is closed.

If you do not choose ‘Unfollow and force-flat’ your position will remain open and you will still be liable for 20% commission payable to the Master.
