How do I deposit money into my account?

You can deposit money into your account via telegraphic transfer, PayPal, Neteller and AIB by visiting ‘Deposit’ on the app.

How do I withdraw funds from my account?

You can withdraw funds at any time by visiting ‘Withdrawal’ on the app. We recommend that you monitor your account closely to ensure you maintain sufficient margin.

How long does it take to transfer funds?

Crediting funds: funds are usually credited to your account instantly.

Withdrawing funds: TigerWit will transmit funds within two days. It may take longer for funds to appear in your bank account depending on your bank’s processing procedure and whether the withdrawal was made on a weekday or weekend.

What is the minimum deposit required?

The minimum deposit amount is USD200.

Are there withdrawal fees?

TigerWit does not usually charge any withdrawal fees
