What is the swap rate?

The swap rate is the interest applied when you hold open positions overnight. No swap rate applies if you open and then close a position during a trading day’s transaction period.

Does the swap rate change for different products?

The swap rate for each currency pair is different for different trading directions.

When is the swap rate applied?

Swap rates will be calculated at 22.00 UTC and TigerWit may update swap rates without notice. If you hold a position open overnight on Wednesday/Thursday, then the swap rate applicable is tripled. This triple charge covers the weekend period and is a market convention routed in settlement times for real forex trades (as opposed to CFDs). Given two day settlement, a trade made on Wednesday evening will be settled on Friday evening and the swap charge applied therefore reflected the weekend.

Where can I check the swap rate quotation?

On the app, visit the product's trading page. Click on the ‘?’ icon to view the swap rate information.

Where can I check the previous swap rate charges?

On the app click ‘Trade’ / ‘History’ / ‘Closed Orders’
