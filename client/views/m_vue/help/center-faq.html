  • Opening an account
    How do I open an account?

    To open a Live or Experience account, download the app and click on ‘Register’.

    Can I open more than one Live account?

    You can have up to seven Live sub-accounts on the same ID.

    Can I open a joint Live account?

    Yes, Please contact Customer Service for more information at customerservice@tigerwit.com.

    How long does it take to open an account?

    You can open an Experience account immediately. Normally a Live account can be set up within minutes although we may ask you for further information as part of the process.

    What information or documents do I need to open an account?

    We will require copies of your passport or your government-issued national ID card with a photo.

  • Trading
    When is the platform open?
    During UK summer time London is UTC plus 1 hour otherwise it is the same as UTC.
    Will you close any of my positions?

    When the margin ratio (margin/free margin) is below 50%, TigerWit will effect liquidation starting from your position showing the highest loss. Please maintain sufficient margin on your account at all times

    What is a price re-quotation and when might it happen?

    The most common reasons for a price re-quotation are:
      •  Volatility: When markets are experiencing high volatility, the price may change dramatically.
      •  Slow connectivity: You may be trading on a poor internet connection which can result in price latency.
    In cases such as these we may offer a revised market quote to you. You can choose to accept the re-quoted price (which may itself then require a re-quote) or cancel the order.

    What is slippage and when might it occur?

    In case of liquidation of a position, or when a stop-loss is triggered, the trade is executed at the actual market price. Immediately following a stop-loss or liquidation being triggered, the actual market price may no longer be at the trigger level. In the case of gaps, the trade will be made at the first available market price following the gap, which may differ from the previously quoted price.

    What is the minimum lot size I can trade?

    The minimum lot size is 0.01 Lot for FX and precious metals and 0.1 Lot for indices and oils

    How can I view the details of the trading products?

    On the app, click on the ‘?’ icon at the top of the trading symbol to view product details.

    What leverage do you offer?

    TigerWit offers leverage 200 : 1 depending on the market, although Crypto Currency (Bitcoin etc) leverage is 2 :1. Higher leverage may be available if you are eligible to be treated as a Professional Client. Please contact Client Support for more information at customerservice@tigerwit.com.

    What trading fees do you charge?

    TigerWit only charges the spread. There is no commission applicable.

    Is there a time limit on holding open positions or orders to open?

    There is no time limit for open orders or positions. There is no limit on the number of orders or positions you can have open at any time so long as you maintain sufficient margin to cover any losses and swap charges.

  • Deposit and withdrawal
    How do I deposit money into my account?

    You can deposit money into your account via telegraphic transfer, PayPal, Neteller, Omipay and AIB by visiting ‘Deposit’ on the app.

    How do I withdraw funds from my account?

    You can withdraw funds at any time by visiting ‘Withdrawal’ on the app. We recommend that you monitor your account closely to ensure you maintain sufficient margin.

    How long does it take to transfer funds?

    Crediting funds: funds are usually credited to your account instantly.

    Withdrawing funds: TigerWit will transmit funds within two days. It may take longer for funds to appear in your bank account depending on your bank’s processing procedure and whether the withdrawal was made on a weekday or weekend.

    What is the minimum deposit required?

    The minimum deposit amount is USD200

    Are there withdrawal fees?

    TigerWit does not usually charge any withdrawal fees

  • Copy trading
    Earning commission

    TigerWit Masters earn 20% of the value of successful trades made by followers. This amount is credited to the Master’s account automatically.

    Paying commission

    Followers pay 20% of the profit made by following a Master’s trade to the Master. Commission is deducted automatically

    Unfollow

    If you choose to unfollow a Master’s trade your position will remain open until it is manually closed or you choose ‘Unfollow and force-flat’. You will still be liable for 20% commission payable to the Master when the position is closed.

    If you do not choose ‘Unfollow and force-flat’ your position will remain open and you will still be liable for 20% commission payable to the Master.

  • Swap rate
    What is the swap rate?

    The swap rate is the interest applied when you hold open positions overnight. No swap rate applies if you open and then close a position during a trading day’s transaction period.

    Does the swap rate change for different products?

    The swap rate for each currency pair is different for different trading directions.

    When is the swap rate applied?

    Swap rates will be calculated at 22.00 UTC and TigerWit may update swap rates without notice. If you hold a position open overnight on Wednesday/Thursday, then the swap rate applicable is tripled. This triple charge covers the weekend period and is a market convention routed in settlement times for real forex trades (as opposed to CFDs). Given two day settlement, a trade made on Wednesday evening will be settled on Friday evening and the swap charge applied therefore reflected the weekend.

    Where can I check the swap rate quotation?

    On the app, visit the product's trading page. Click on the ‘?’ icon to view the swap rate information.

    Where can I check the previous swap rate charges?

    On the app click ‘Trade’ / ‘History’ / ‘Closed Orders’
